Features

Attainable health and wellness is what NutriFit is all about

Whether you're a beginner wanting to take the first step toward a healthy lifestyle or a seasoned pro looking to shake up their workout routine, we want to help you reach your fitness and nutritional goals. That's why we developed this all-in-one, user-friendly web app and packed it full of features. Discover the best of what NutriFit has to offer!

Meal Logging

Organize and log meals and view nutritional values for specific foods such as fat content, protein, and calories.

Workout Planning

Browse workouts by targeted muscle group, view workout instructions, and track calories burned each workout session.

Goal Tracking

Set and track progress toward fitness and nutritional goals and view your stats throughout your fitness journey.
